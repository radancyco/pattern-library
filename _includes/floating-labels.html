Accessibility & UX

Including labels that sit outside of form fields is always encouraged and a more robust and fully accessible solution when it comes to the design of any form. Including static labels on form fields is particularly crucial in the design of complex forms. While we do allow use of floating labels under certain conditions, please familiraize yourself with the pitfalls of leveraging them before designing or implementing them on your next project.

  • Floating labels are problematic by Adam Silver
  • Gone Floating Labels And Green Lighthouse Scores by Vitaly Friedman

Note: Substituting labels with placeholder text is ill-advised. Understand the difference between a proper label and placeholder text.
