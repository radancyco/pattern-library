Accessibility & UX

Including form outside of input fields is always encouraged and a more robust and fully accessible solution when it comes to the design of any form. Including proper labels is especially important in the design of complex forms. While we do allow use of floating labels, please familiraize yourself with the pitfalls of leveraging them before designing or implementing them on your next project.
