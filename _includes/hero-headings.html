Accessibility: Hero Banner Headings

Before using an h1 heading in your hero banner, please make absolutely sure a heading is what you really need. Ask yourself these questions:

  • Is the hero banner meant as more of a call to action or branded message? If so, you may wish to treat the banner as decorative, in which case a div should be used instead of an h1 or lower heading level.
  • Is there another element on the page that is better suited to be a primary heading? Often, this will be below the hero banner. If so, this should be your h1.
  • Think contextually. Often, the hero banner on the homepage could justifiably describe the overarching message of the site and be an h1, while hero banners on subpages may demand a div, especially if those suppages include a more suitable headings that can be your h1.
